Frequently asked questions

What is AI GOD?

A native macOS app that lets you chat with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google and OpenRouter from a single window. You bring your own API key (BYOK), so the provider bills you directly for what you use — usually a few cents a day.

Do my conversations leave my Mac?

Only the actual message you send goes to the AI provider you chose. Chat history, preferences and API keys stay on your Mac. We have no server in the middle. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What does the 15-day trial include?

Full functionality, no limits, no watermark. After 15 days the app prompts for a license. The Mac App Store build has no trial because Apple handles entitlement directly.

How do I activate a license?

After buying on Gumroad you will receive an email with a license inside a BEGIN/END block. Open AI GOD → trial overlay → Paste your license → paste the block → Activate. The license binds to this Mac and works offline forever.

Can I use the license on more than one Mac?

Yes. The license is tied to your email, not to a hardware ID. Activate it on as many of your personal Macs as you want.

How do I get an API key?

  • OpenAI: platform.openai.com/api-keys
  • Anthropic: console.anthropic.com/settings/keys
  • Google AI Studio: aistudio.google.com/apikey
  • OpenRouter (one key for many models): openrouter.ai/keys

"The app is damaged and cannot be opened"

The download was probably corrupted by your browser. Re-download the DMG, then run in Terminal:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/AI\ GOD.app

Microphone does not work

Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and make sure AI GOD is allowed. If it is not listed, launch AI GOD and click the mic button once; macOS will ask for permission.

"Invalid API key" when I send a message

Open Settings → API Keys inside the app and re-paste the key. Verify on the provider's website that the key is still active and has credit available.

Why does AI GOD need a microphone entitlement?

Only if you use the voice-to-text button (Whisper transcription). The microphone is never accessed unless you press that button.

Will AI GOD work without an internet connection?

The UI launches and your previous conversations open offline, but sending a new message requires reaching the AI provider you chose. License activation happens once, then re-verification is fully offline.

I lost my license file — what now?

Open a support ticket above with your Gumroad order ID and we will re-issue it within 24 hours.

System requirements

  • macOS 12.0 (Monterey) or later
  • Intel or Apple Silicon (universal binary)
  • An API key from at least one supported provider
