AI GOD — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 May 2026 · Last updated: 26 May 2026

TL;DR

AI GOD is a desktop application that runs entirely on your Mac. We do not collect, transmit, or store any user data. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no third-party tracking.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone, IP, device identifiers)
  • Your conversations — they live only on your Mac
  • API keys — stored locally, encrypted in macOS Keychain
  • Usage analytics (clicks, time-on-app, feature usage)
  • Crash reports — logs are written only to ~/Library/Application Support/AI GOD/aigod.log and are never uploaded
  • Cookies or browser fingerprints

How the app works

AI GOD runs a local Express backend bound exclusively to 127.0.0.1 (loopback). The Electron UI talks to this backend over the loopback interface only. No external listener is exposed.

Two optional network connections may occur, both initiated by you:

  1. AI provider requests (only when you send a message): direct HTTPS request to the provider you chose (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, OpenRouter), authenticated with your own API key. The request goes from your Mac straight to the provider — we are not in the middle, we never see it.
  2. License activation (Gumroad / Developer ID build only): one-time Ed25519 signature verification. The license file you paste is checked locally on your Mac. The Mac App Store build does not need a license — Apple manages entitlement instead.

Where your data lives

All data AI GOD creates lives in:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/AI GOD/ — local user profile, chat history (db.json), preferences
  • ~/Library/Containers/com.aigod.app/Data/... — same data in sandboxed form for the Mac App Store build
  • macOS Keychain — encrypted API keys, license token

None of this data is ever uploaded, synced, or shared.

Third-party AI providers

When you send a message, the request is made directly from your Mac to the AI provider you selected. Their privacy policy applies to that request:

  • OpenAI
  • Anthropic
  • Google
  • OpenRouter

Children's privacy

AI GOD is a productivity tool for adults and is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal data, we cannot inadvertently collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the architecture of the app changes. Any update will increase the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Material changes will also be announced in the in-app release notes.
